Rainbow Googles

Make the web a bit more colorful

The web isn't colorful enough. This project solves that. Simply drag this button to your bookmarks' page and, when you're in the mood for a little bit of color, click it. It'll add rainbows to any page.

Drag this to your bookmarks bar:

Rainbow Googles

Why?

On Friday, the 26th of June 2015, the american gay rights movement had a major victory: the Supreme Court ruled that their Constitution guarantees a right to same-sex marriage. The news spread over the Internet in the moments following this decision, with many people commemorating it.

Soon after, the White House added the LGBT's rainbow flag to its Facebook profile image. Facebook developed a page where you could do the same to your picture, then many followed. Rainbows were everywhere, but only inside Facebook. This project brings them to the entire web.
